About Strathclyde Business School
Strathclyde Business School is Scotland's most established business faculty with particular strength in accounting and strategy. Triple Crown accredited with strong industry connections, Strathclyde offers practical, professionally-oriented business education in Scotland's largest city with good employability outcomes. Founded in 1948, it was the first business school in Scotland.
