About King's Business School
King's College London is a prestigious Russell Group university with a rapidly growing business school. Located on the iconic Strand campus in central London, King's Business School offers modern programmes combining rigorous academics with strong industry access. The school benefits from King's historic reputation while building a dynamic presence in business education across finance, consulting, tech, and creative sectors.
