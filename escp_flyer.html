About the Programme
The ESCP Bachelor in Management (BSc) is a multi-campus European programme combining management foundations, liberal arts, quantitative skills and languages, with required internships and social impact projects. Delivered across multiple campuses, it culminates in the French Grade de Licence.
Campuses & Mobility
Students rotate yearly between Paris, London, Berlin, Madrid, Turin, and Warsaw. Year 1 typically begins in Paris or London, Year 2 in Berlin or Turin, and Year 3 in Madrid or Warsaw. Academic partnerships extend mobility options to institutions like Bocconi, ESADE, and LSE.
