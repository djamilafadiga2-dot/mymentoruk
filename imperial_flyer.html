About Imperial
Imperial College London is one of the world's leading STEM universities. Its Business School offers analytically rigorous programmes that combine finance, data science, and innovation, ideal for students seeking quantitative finance, fintech, or entrepreneurship careers. Located in South Kensington, central London, Imperial provides access to world-class facilities and strong connections to both finance and technology sectors.
