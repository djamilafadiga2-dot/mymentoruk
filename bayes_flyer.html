About Bayes Business School
Bayes Business School (formerly Cass) is a highly career-oriented business school specializing in finance, insurance, and quantitative fields. Located in the heart of London's financial district near Liverpool Street and Moorgate, Bayes offers practical, industry-focused education with strong connections to the City's financial institutions. Triple Crown accredited (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS).
