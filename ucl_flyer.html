About UCL
UCL is a prestigious Russell Group university with a rapidly growing reputation in business and management education. The UCL School of Management offers innovative programmes combining strategy, innovation, and quantitative analysis, positioning graduates for diverse business careers. Located in historic Bloomsbury, central London, UCL provides excellent interdisciplinary opportunities and access to London's business ecosystem.
